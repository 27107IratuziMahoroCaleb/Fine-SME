  FINE SME — SME Financial Sustainability Prediction System Using
  Transaction Data

  Thematic Area: FinTech / AI / Business Analytics / SME Risk &
  Sustainability Intelligence
  Context: Rwanda-focused academic prototype for innovation center
  evaluation; targets Commercial Banks, MFIs, SACCOs, and SME support
  programs.

  The Problem

  SMEs drive employment and growth but suffer from cash-flow instability
   and poor access to credit. Lenders rely on periodic financial
  statements that are often late, incomplete, or missing entirely.

  Core Idea

  Build an AI-driven system that ingests transaction data (sales
  inflows, expenses, payment patterns, balances) and predicts:
  - Short-term liquidity stress (30/60/90 days)
  - Long-term financial sustainability (6–12 months)
